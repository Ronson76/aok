# 🧱 AOK Homeless Data Capture App (Lean + Compliant)

This is staff-led. Not service-user dependent.

---

# 1️⃣ Core Structure (One Interaction = One Record)

Every time staff engage with a person, they log a structured record.

### Section A – Identity

* Individual ID / Name
* Programme (Outreach / Hostel / Drop-in)
* Assigned Worker

---

### Section B – Contact Type

* Outreach visit
* Shelter check-in (staff confirmed)
* Drop-in meeting
* Phone contact
* Multi-agency discussion

Timestamp auto-logged.
Location auto-logged (if device-enabled).

---

### Section C – Safeguarding Risk Status

Risk Tier (mandatory):

* High
* Medium
* Low

Risk Indicators (tick-box only):

* Rough sleeping
* Exploitation risk
* Domestic abuse
* Substance misuse
* Mental health concern
* Violence risk
* Self-harm concern
* Missing contact pattern

No free-text essays unless necessary.

Structured data > narratives.

---

### Section D – Action Taken

Mandatory selection:

* Advice provided
* Referral made (select agency)
* Emergency accommodation arranged
* DSL informed
* Safeguarding referral submitted
* No action required
* Follow-up planned

If “Safeguarding referral submitted” selected → escalation log triggered.

---

### Section E – Follow-Up Requirement

* Follow-up required? (Yes / No)
* Review date
* Responsible staff member

System automatically tracks overdue follow-ups.

---

# 2️⃣ Safeguarding Compliance Layer (Built In)

To meet safeguarding expectations:

The app must automatically:

* Time-stamp every entry
* Record staff identity
* Prevent deletion (archive only)
* Maintain full audit trail
* Log escalation decisions
* Record rationale for “no action” in high-risk cases

No silent edits.
No missing accountability.

That’s what inspectors look for.

---

# 3️⃣ Shelter Tablet Version (Simplified Input)

If used at shelter:

Two modes:

### Mode 1 – Staff Assisted

Staff logs full structured interaction (as above).

### Mode 2 – Self Check-In (Minimal)

Service user enters:

* Name / ID
* “Request to see worker?” Yes / No

That creates a “contact logged” entry.

But risk tier is not updated automatically.

Only staff update risk.

That keeps safeguarding clean.

---

# 4️⃣ Lost Contact Flagging Logic

At intake:

Risk tier determines expected contact frequency.

If no interaction logged within timeframe:

* Flag appears in staff dashboard
* Outreach attempt required
* Attempt must be logged

This creates documented safeguarding oversight.

---

# 5️⃣ What This App Is NOT

It is not:

* A crisis line
* A live monitoring tool
* A predictive AI engine
* A replacement for statutory systems

It is:

**Structured safeguarding interaction capture with compliance integrity.**

---

# 6️⃣ What Makes It Legally Safer

To protect AOK and the organisation:

* Clear disclaimer: Not emergency monitoring
* Clear escalation responsibility sits with organisation
* Role-based permissions (worker / manager / DSL)
* Data retention controls aligned to policy
* Secure login per staff member

That’s governance-ready.
