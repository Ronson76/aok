Our Proactive Safeguarding Model

A-OK creates a live, organisation-level safeguarding timeline.

1. Early Visibility

Structured check-ins confirm wellbeing, location, and activity in real time.

2. Risk Identification

Missed check-ins and behavioural changes are automatically flagged.

3. Controlled Escalation

Alerts follow a defined pathway — ensuring the right people are informed at the right time.

4. Multi-Agency Response

Authorised partners can be included in the safeguarding loop.

5. Full Audit Trail

Every action is securely time-stamped and exportable.

This moves safeguarding from:

Reactive → Preventative
Assumed → Evidenced
Manual → Automated
